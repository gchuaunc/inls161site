Interests

A little bit about some of the things I'm interested in. Each item is linked to a sub-page where you can read more!


Programming open_in_new

I first learned how to program in middle school. My first language was FreeBASIC, a version of Microsoft qBASIC compiled for Windows. Throughout the years, I've created dozens of apps, utilities, and other programs to help me and those around me in some small way.

Game development open_in_new

Everyone's played games, but creating them is something else. I enjoy creating games, especially in the context of game jams or competitions. Having some kind of arbitrary constraint, like a theme or a time limit, encourages me to finish the game and polish it as well as I can.

Bike fix and flip open_in_new

In 2022, I fixed up a bike for the first time after buying one from the UNC bicycle auction. After realizing I could do this (and what a crazy steal of a deal I got!), I was hooked and have since been buying bikes that need work and fixing them to be sold on to a good home.

Cycling and MTB open_in_new

I enjoy cycling with friends and family as well as learning new skills (and doing crazy things) with my mountain bike.
