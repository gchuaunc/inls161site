The Desert Island question

If I could bring any ten items to a desert island where I would be stranded, what would they be?

  1. Multitool. Don't underestimate a good multitool. I love my Swiss Army knife.
  2. Bucket. For collecting rainwater. Don't want to dehydrate!
  3. A large net. Maybe for catching fish, or for building a shelter.
  4. Durable shoes. I might need to climb a tree or something. These shoes should last a while.
  5. Rope / paracord. For building things like shelters.
  6. First aid kit. Just in case. I will probably need it.
  7. Axe. Cut trees and things.
  8. Duct tape. A great all-round bandaid fix.
  9. Long-lasting firestarter, like flint and steel.
  10. Survival manual. I can't remember how to do this stuff! I need this manual.
